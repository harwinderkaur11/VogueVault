Makeup Aesthetic

Learn how to create a dreamy pastel makeup aesthetic to enhance your style and confidence.

Step 1: Start with a Clean Base

Begin with a fresh, moisturized face. Apply a lightweight foundation or BB cream for an even and natural-looking base.

Step 2: Soft Pastel Eyes

Choose pastel shades like lilac, baby pink, or mint for your eyelids. Blend softly for a dreamy and subtle effect.

Step 3: Flawless Glow

Highlight your cheekbones, brow bones, and the bridge of your nose. Use a gentle shimmer to achieve a natural glow.

Step 4: Play with Lips

Opt for pastel lipsticks or lip gloss in soft pinks, peaches, or lavender. You can also layer with shimmer gloss for added aesthetic.

Step 5: Finishing Touches

Define your lashes with a subtle mascara. Keep the overall look soft and ethereal. The key to makeup aesthetic is balance and harmony of colors.
