Nail Art

Explore trendy nail art designs and easy DIY tips for every occasion.

Step 1: Prepare Your Nails

Start with clean nails. Trim, file, and buff them to create a smooth surface for polish application.

Step 2: Apply Base Coat

A base coat protects your nails from staining and makes your nail art last longer.

Step 3: Choose Your Colors

Select pastel shades or bold colors based on your aesthetic. Use complementary colors for patterns or designs.

Step 4: Add Designs

Use nail art brushes, dotting tools, or even tape to create patterns like stripes, dots, or geometric shapes.

Step 5: Finish with Top Coat

Seal your design with a top coat to add shine and prevent chipping.
